铭记过去，展望未来  
Take the once and look the future

跨越无数次的孤独与迷茫，在黑暗中挣扎与不甘  
Across loneliness and confusion. Struggling and unwilling in the dark

牵动光的翼羽，去找寻那风的黎明  
Pick the wings of light to find the dawn of wind  
_——Free Wind And Light_

_CTS By Wuqibor_
